I am a second-year master's student in Department of Electrical Engineering at Columbia University. I am interested in Robot Data-Driven Control in Dynamic Environments, Robotic Representation Learning, Multi-modality Learning, and Computer Vision. My research attempts to teach robots to better explore and understand the world by themselves so that in the future they will become not only smarter assistants but also trustworthy friends 🤖.

I received my Bachelor's degree of Electrical and Automation Engineering from Nanjing Normal University (2020), and I was a short-term student at Rice University (2021). Now I am studying in Columbia University and expecting to graduate on Feb 2023.

I was a research assistant in Robot Learning and Control Group of Nanjing University for Professor Chunlin Chen and the leader of UAV Communication Link Denoising Group in Jiangsu Electromagnetic Compatibility Engineering with Professor Wei Yan. I was also working in Kodiak Robotics as a deep learning intern in 2022 summer. I am now the member of Columbia Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Lab advised by Professor Shuran Song.
